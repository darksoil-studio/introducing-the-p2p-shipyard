p2p Shipyard

A full-stack development suite for p2p apps

Why?

- p2p apps have unique capabilities: - Censorship resistance. - They work even without internet access. - Transparency and immutability.
### But! - They can be really difficult to build. - Often feels like your swimming upstream.

p2p Shipyard

- Full stack development suite, including: - Plug & play modules for commonly needed features. - Bridges to connect to centralized services. - Support for both desktop and mobile platforms. - Uses Holochain as its underlying p2p protocol. - Uses Tauri as the packaging tooling.

Demo

- Go to [https://darksoil.studio/demo-launcher](https://darksoil.studio/demo-launcher) to download our app store for demos.

Roadmap

- Currently in beta. - Services managed from a web console interface: - Push notifications. - Locker: encrypted backup for p2p apps. - Email notifications. - Stripe integration. - OS support: - iOS. - Volla Cloud integration!
